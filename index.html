About Me

Quality engineering meets
AI innovation.

I'm Thomas Wrappe — a Software Development Engineer in Test with 3+ years at Dolby Laboratories, where I build automation frameworks, CI/CD infrastructure, fuzz testing harnesses, and system-level validation pipelines for performance-critical media and firmware technologies.

My work spans C++ fuzz testing with AFL++ and LLVM SanitizerCoverage for embedded firmware, Dolby Vision certification automation, pixel-level picture quality validation, and AI-augmented QA tooling — including LLM-powered log analyzers and RAG-based CI/CD failure analysis agents.

I partner closely with globally distributed engineering teams to shift quality left, accelerate release cycles, and protect the end-user experience across hardware and software boundaries.
